# MoonEscape

## 🚀 Introduction
Welcome to **MoonEscape**, a 2D space survival shooter built for the web. Created as a school project, this game puts players in the cockpit of a lone starship navigating the treacherous void of space. Your mission is to survive the onslaught of enemy drones, dodge incoming attacks, and escape the moon's gravity well.

## 📖 The Lore
In the distant future, lunar colonies have been abandoned and overrun by rogue autonomous defense systems. You play as a pilot separated from your fleet during a routine recon mission. With enemies closing in, you must rely on your reflexes and your ship's laser cannons to blast your way to safety.

## 🎮 Gameplay & Mechanics
**MoonEscape** captures the feel of classic arcade shoot-'em-ups using vanilla web mechanics. 
* **Dynamic Combat:** Engage enemy ships and navigate around their attack patterns.
* **Precision Controls:** Maneuver your ship to dodge incoming fire and obstacles.
* **Firepower:** Utilize your ship's lasers to destroy rogue drones and clear a path forward.
* **Health Management:** Monitor your ship's integrity. Collect HP restore bonuses dropped in the battlefield to repair your ship and stay in the fight.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Parallax Scrolling:** A multi-layered visual environment with a scrolling moon background to create a sense of speed and depth.
* **Progressive Difficulty:** As you survive longer, the enemy waves become more frequent and challenging.
* **Interactive UI:** A custom graphical user interface featuring stylized health bars, animated menus, and modal windows.
* **Sound Integration:** Fully integrated audio system (`sound.js`) featuring background music, laser blasts, and sound effects for a complete arcade experience.

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture
**MoonEscape** was built from scratch without the use of heavy game engines or external frameworks:
* **Core Engine:** Written entirely in **Vanilla JavaScript**, utilizing a standard game loop architecture (`game.js`) for rendering and state management.
* **Modular Codebase:** The logic is separated into distinct, organized files for specific game entities (e.g., `player.js`, `enemies.js`, `lasers.js`).
* **Styling & Animation:** UI layout and animations are powered entirely by custom **CSS3** (`game.css`, `menu.css`, `style.css`).
* **Asset Management:** Handles loading and managing custom sprites, parallax background layers, and audio files.

## 🎓 About This Project
**MoonEscape** was developed as a school project to demonstrate proficiency in web development, specifically focusing on Vanilla JavaScript, CSS styling, and game logic implementation in the browser. As this is a completed academic assignment, development has concluded and there are no planned future updates.
